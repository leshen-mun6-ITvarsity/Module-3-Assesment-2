Steak

Prep Time: 8 Minutes

Yield: 5 Minutes

INGREDIANTS

STEPS

  1. Let the steak come to room temprature for atleast 5 minutes.
  2. Season the steak on both sides with salt and butter, heat the skillet or grill to 145 F/63 C.
  3. Sear the steak with melted butter, each side should be seared for 3 to 4 minutes.
