Cupcake

Prep Time: 30 Minutes

Yield: 5 1/2 dozen

INGREDIANTS

STEPS

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, cinnamon, butter and milk well in a bowl untill homogeneous.
  2. place in cupcake tray and place in the oven at 180 degrees C.
  3. Let them bake for 30 to 35 minutes, after you timer is up let them cool on a wire rack.
