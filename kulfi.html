Mango Kulfi

Prep Time: 8 hours

Yield: 10

INGREDIANTS

STEPS

  1. Add all ingrediants into a blender and blend untill smooth.
  2. Add the mixture to an ice cream mold tray(can also put it in a bowl) and put it in the freezer for at least 8 hours(4 hours if in a bowl).
  3. Its ready to eat ass soon as its done setting in the freezer.
